New Flame Power Formula

Individual Axies' Flame = Base Atia's Flame + Evolved Part Boost + Accessory Boost

Your Plot's Flame = ⌊ Total of ALL Working Axies' Flame

  × ( 1 + Land Item % Boost × Environment Multiplier )

  × ( 1 + Fortune Slips Buff ) ⌋

bAXS per tick = ( Your Plot's Flame ÷ Total Flame of all mining plots in your category ) × Total bAXS pool for that category

All three components stack multiplicatively on the plot level.
